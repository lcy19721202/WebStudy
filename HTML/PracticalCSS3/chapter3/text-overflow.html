My affable little troll

My troll is a friendly, charming & most agreeable little chap, with a heart of gold. Pastimes

When not out helping those in need, my troll likes to kick back and relax. His favourite hobby is the delicate art of antidisestablishmentarialism.

Diet

My troll can eat a variety of foods, and in fact is not fussy at all: he will sample anything, from the finest caviar and oysters of the Savoy, to the best steak Texas has to offer! His favourite foods however, are rotten shark and used stamps.

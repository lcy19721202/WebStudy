My affable little troll

My troll is a friendly, charming & most affable little chap with a diminutive size, but a heart of gold. He likes nothing more than to help people in need, whether we are talking about an old lady who wants to cross the street, a child who has lost his favourite toy, or a supervillain for whom world domination is within grasp â€” save for a vital component currently in possession of one of his archrivals.

Ok, so he may get it wrong sometimes, and be a bit too trusting (some would say he is afflicted with a gullible nature), but his heart is always in the right place. He just needs a gentle push sometimes, in the direction of the good guys!

Pastimes

When not out helping those in need, my troll likes to kick back and relax. This usually involves go karting, bungee jumping, ice skating, horse racing, mud wrestling, fencing, karate lessons, and playing guitar in a rock band. Most people are very impressed by his wide range of pastimes, as long as he doesn't try to do of them at the same time.

Diet

My troll can eat a variety of foods, and in fact is not fussy at all: he scoffs anything, from the finest caviar and oysters of the Savoy, to the best steak Texas has to offer! His favourite foods however, are rotten shark, mouldy Bull Yoghurt™ and used stamps.

Troll Maths

Favourite troll sums include 97 + 1 = 100, 2/3 + 1/3 = 6, and 23 × 2 = 47. My troll was 1st in his class this year, which is great considering last year he was 43rd.

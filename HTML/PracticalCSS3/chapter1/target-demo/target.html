:target

Here, the :target pseudo-class is used to create CSS-only tabbed navigation. <article> "tabs" are absolutely positioned on top of one another and given opacity: 0; , then a transition is used to make tabs smoothly appear when the links that target them are clicked, i.e. when they become a target ( article[id*=tab]:target {} ).

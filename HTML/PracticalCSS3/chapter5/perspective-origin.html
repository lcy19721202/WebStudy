Visit my shop

Ladies and gentleman, you are welcome to visit my shop and peruse its wares, if you are ever able to find it. You see, it is a magic shop: it only appears to beings of pure heart in times of dire need. Well, ok, it also appears to evil souls in times of need, and generally ends up selling them the wrong item, or something broken, or something in which the batteries have run out. And, you can often find it in a random place in Covent Garden on every 3rd Sunday of the month. And occasionally selling out of the back of a van, in Düsseldorf city center. But only if the cafe opposite is serving toast.
